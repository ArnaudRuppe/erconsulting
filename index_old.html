Our Specialized Services

We offer comprehensive solutions tailored to the unique needs of wineries and wine production facilities.

Wine Hygiene Solutions

Comprehensive hygiene programs designed specifically for wineries, including sanitation protocols, equipment cleaning procedures, and staff training to maintain the highest standards of cleanliness throughout production.

Winery Audits

Detailed facility audits to assess compliance with industry standards, identify potential contamination risks, and provide actionable recommendations to improve your wine quality and production efficiency.

Biofilm Treatment

Advanced biofilm detection and elimination services using cutting-edge technology and specialized treatments to remove persistent microbial communities that can compromise wine quality and safety.

About ERConsulting

Founded by industry experts with decades of experience in wine microbiology and production, ERConsulting brings scientific rigor and practical solutions to wineries of all sizes.

We understand the unique challenges of maintaining hygiene in wine production environments and have developed specialized approaches that balance effectiveness with the delicate nature of wine products.

Our team combines academic expertise with hands-on winery experience to deliver solutions that are both scientifically sound and practically implementable in real-world production settings.
